We're bringing first generation of fighting robots. 
Level up system, team battles, variety of characters and more to come.

### Mission

Kaibots' mission is to bring fun to people through robot toys. We are working to integrate many available techologies to create a new kind of fun. 

We combine three things:

* Videogames
* Robotics
* Smartphones


### How do we combine these three?

Robots to play with. Kaibots are robots you can control with your smartphone to play with others using videogame mechanics!
